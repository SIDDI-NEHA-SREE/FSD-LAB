About Campus Event Management System

The Campus Event Management System is designed to streamline the organization and management of events on campus. It provides a platform for students, faculty, and staff to create, manage, and participate in various campus events efficiently.

Our system offers features such as event creation, registration, notifications, and feedback collection to enhance the overall event experience for all participants.
